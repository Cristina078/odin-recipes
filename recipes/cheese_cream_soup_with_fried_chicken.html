Cheese Cream Soup With Fried Chicken

Description of Cheese Cream Soup With Fried Chicken

The soup is cooked in chicken broth, which makes it more nutritious. The use of cream, cream and hard cheese gives the soup an incredible tenderness. Pieces of boiled chicken are fried until golden brown, they are supplemented with cheese cream soup, which makes the dish even more interesting and fragrant.

Ingredients for Cheese Cream Soup With Fried Chicken

  • Chicken breast - 350 g
  • Potatoes - 180 g
  • Cream cheese - 200 g
  • Hard cheese - 50 g
  • Cream with a fat content of 33 % - 30 milliliters
  • Carrots - 100 g
  • Onion - 60 g
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Vegetable oil - 20 milliliters
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Water - 1.5 liters

Steps for making Cream Cheese Soup With Fried Chicken

  1. Prepare all the necessary ingredients.
  2. Put the chicken in a saucepan with a thick bottom, pour water, salt and bring to a boil. Cook for 30 minutes over low heat.Then put the chicken on a plate and cool. Then remove the skin and remove the chicken meat from the bone.
  3. Cut the chicken meat into cubes. Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan and fry for 5 minutes over medium heat. Then transfer to a plate.
  4. Peel garlic and onion and finely chop. Put the vegetables in a frying pan with the oil remaining after frying the chicken meat.
  5. Peel the carrots and cut them into small cubes. Add the vegetables to the pan.
  6. Peel the potatoes, cut into small cubes and add to the pan.
  7. Fry everything for 15 minutes on medium heat, stirring.
  8. Bring the chicken broth to a boil, put the fried vegetables from the pan. Cook the soup for 10 minutes.
  9. Add cream cheese to the soup and stir.
  10. Grate the hard cheese on a fine grater and add to the pan, mix.
  11. Pour the cream into the pan.
  12. Bring the soup to a boil, add salt and pepper to taste, cook on low heat for another 5 minutes. Then remove the pan from the heat.
  13. Punch the soup with a blender until smooth.
  14. Pour the soup on plates, add the fried chicken and serve to the table. Enjoy your meal.
