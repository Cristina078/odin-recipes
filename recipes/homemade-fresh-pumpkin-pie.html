Homemade Fresh Pumpkin Pie

Description of Homemade Pumpkin Pie

Simple and quick to make pumpkin pie with condensed milk turns out to be very tender and delicious. A crumbly sandy base and a delicate souffle are a great option for a home tea party. Condensed milk gives the cake a light caramel note, and pumpkin makes it bright and healthy. The pie is designed for 8 servings.

Ingredients of Homemade Pumpkin Pie

  • Pumpkin - 450-500 g
  • Condensed milk - 1 jar (380 g)
  • Butter - 125 g
  • Chicken youlks 2 pieces
  • Chicken egg 1 pieces
  • Flour - 250 g
  • Sugar - 70 g
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Ginger

Steps for making homemade pumpkin pie

  1. Prepare the ingredients for pumpkin pie.
  2. Sift wheat flour into a bowl, add butter. Stir until the mass turns into a fine crumb.
  3. Add sugar and chicken yolks.
  4. Quickly knead the shortbread dough, wrap it with cling film and place it in the refrigerator for 30-40 minutes.
  5. We prepare a pumpkin souffle. Peel the pumpkin, cut it into arbitrary pieces and put it in a saucepan. Pour 50-70 ml of water, cook over low heat for 20-30 minutes.
  6. We put the pieces of stewed pumpkin in a blender (without liquid), bring the mass to the state of puree.
  7. Add condensed milk, egg and spices.
  8. Mix until smooth.
  9. We put the shortbread dough in a baking dish, in the form of a base cake, forming the sides.
  10. Pour the pumpkin-milk mixture on the base.
  11. Place in a preheated oven and bake a pumpkin souffle pie for 15 minutes at 200 degrees. Reduce the temperature to 170 degrees, wait another 30-35 minutes.
  12. Completely cooled pumpkin pie with condensed milk is cut into portions. Enjoy your meal.
